SECTION C (20 marks)

Answer one question only from this section on the lined answer sheets on pages 5 to 6. Write between 150 and 200 words.

EITHER (Question 21)

Imagine that you have passed the Primary School Leaving Certificate Examination and that you have been selected to start Form 1 at Kabwabwa Secondary School. Your friends have told you to join a Sports team but you strongly believe that your parents may not be in support of this idea. Write a letter to your parents explaining why you want to join a Sports team.

In your letter you may include the following points:

  • Any three reasons why you want to join the sports team.
  • Any two ways in which sports can be beneficial to you.
  • Any two ways how you will manage to balance your lessons and sports without affecting your academic performance.

OR (Question 22)

Imagine that you have witnessed a number of cases of child abuse at your school. Write a composition on school dropout.

In your composition you may include the following points:

  • Any two causes of child abuse.
  • Any three effects of child abuse.
  • What can be done to stop the problem.
