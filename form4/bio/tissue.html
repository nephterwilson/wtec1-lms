Tissue Respiration

Understanding Cellular Energy Production

1 What is Tissue Respiration?

Tissue respiration is a complex biochemical process occurring within cells that involves the breakdown of glucose to release energy.

Primary Product

ATP (Adenosine Triphosphate) - readily available chemical energy

Secondary Product

Heat energy as a byproduct
