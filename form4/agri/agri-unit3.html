3 Main Drainage Methods

1. Surface Drainage

Removing water from the soil surface

Examples: Open ditches, cambered fields, pumping

2. Subsurface Drainage

Using underground pipes to drain water below soil

More expensive but very effective

3. Biodrainage

Using deep-rooted trees to absorb excess water

Natural and eco-friendly approach
