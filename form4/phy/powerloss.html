Methods of Reducing Transformer Losses

üîß

Engineering Solutions

Several engineering practices can minimize power losses in transformers and improve efficiency.

‚úÖ

Improved Magnetic Coupling

Wind the secondary coil directly over the primary coil for stronger magnetic coupling and reduced leakage flux

‚úÖ

Soft Iron Cores

Use soft iron cores that are easily magnetized and demagnetized to reduce hysteresis losses

‚úÖ

Low-Resistance Wires

Employ low-resistance copper wires to reduce heat loss from electrical resistance in the windings

‚úÖ

Laminated Core

Construct the core with laminated and insulated sheets to reduce eddy current losses

üí°

Key Principle

By implementing these methods, transformer efficiency can be improved from typical values of 95-98% to even higher levels, saving energy and reducing operational costs.
