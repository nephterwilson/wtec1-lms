Why Systematic Planning Matters

A well-designed investigation ensures that results are reliable, valid, and reproducible. By following the scientific method, researchers can minimize bias, control variables effectively, and draw meaningful conclusions from their data.

Each step builds upon the previous one, creating a logical framework that guides the entire investigation from initial question to final conclusion.
