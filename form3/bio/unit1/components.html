üîÑ How Components Interact

Energy Flow

Physical factors like sunlight enable plants to produce energy through photosynthesis. This energy flows through animal communities as they consume plants and each other.

Nutrient Cycling

Physical factors like soil and water provide nutrients. Animals and decomposers break down organic matter, returning nutrients to the physical environment for plants to use again.

Habitat Formation

Physical factors create the foundation for habitats. Plant communities modify these habitats, providing shelter and food for animal communities.

Adaptation & Evolution

Physical factors drive natural selection. Both plant and animal communities develop adaptations that allow them to thrive in specific environmental conditions.
