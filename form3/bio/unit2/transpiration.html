Factors Affecting the Rate of Transpiration

Temperature

Higher temperatures increase evaporation and speed up transpiration.

Humidity

High humidity slows transpiration, while low humidity increases it.

Air Movement

Wind removes moist air near leaves, speeding up water loss.

Light Intensity

Bright light opens stomata, increasing water vapour loss.

Water Supply

Limited water causes stomatal closure, reducing transpiration.
