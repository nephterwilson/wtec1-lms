Stem Structure

Dicotyledonous Stems

Vascular bundles arranged in a ring

Examples: Sunflowers, beans, roses

Monocotyledonous Stems

Vascular bundles scattered throughout

Examples: Corn, grasses, palms
üå±

Root Structure

Dicotyledonous Roots

Xylem occupies center (pith)

Examples: Carrots, turnips, radishes

Monocotyledonous Roots

Vascular bundles in a ring

Examples: Onions, grasses, wheat
