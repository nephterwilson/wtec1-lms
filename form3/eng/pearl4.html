The Pearl

Character Analysis

9. The Doctor's Servant

(i) He is deceitful

He lied that the doctor had gone out when he knew that Kino had no money.

(ii) He is treacherous

He treats his own native, in an inhumane way during the greatest need of help.

(iii) He is selfish

He only responds to him in their own local language and he responds in a foreign language.

10. Other Fishermen (Pearl Divers)

(a) They are caring

They run quickly so to Kino's direction the moment they hear the strange voice.

11. The Shopkeeper

(i) He is selfish

He thinks of the clothes he will sell and the money he is going to realize (make) from Kino.

(ii) He is greedy

He thinks of accumulating wealth for himself.

12. The Trackers

(i) They are greedy

They attack Kino because of his pearl that he wants to sell to them.

(ii) Evil minded

They want to kill Kino and his family because of the Pearl they are possessing.

(iii) They are cunning

They still follow Kino as he is greedy to sell the Pearl to the city.
