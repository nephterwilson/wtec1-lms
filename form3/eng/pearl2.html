üí° Key Insights

Juan Tom√°s represents wisdom and family loyalty. As Kino's brother, he is the only one who stands by him despite his reservations about the pearl. His cleverness is shown in how he helps Kino's family secretly.

The French Doctor embodies the exploitation and prejudice of the colonial system. His numerous negative traits - from racism to greed to trickery - make him a clear antagonist who represents everything wrong with the social hierarchy.

Juana is the moral center of the story. Despite her husband's obsession with the pearl, she remains loving, supportive, and wise. She recognizes the evil the pearl brings and tries to protect her family, showing strength, courage, and remarkable resourcefulness.
